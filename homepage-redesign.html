Three disagreements that ride the whole page

These do not live in a single block. They are the only real arguments once the section-level diagnosis is agreed.

  • How long the page runs. We compress with tabs, toggles, and disclosure. The brief leans on a longer template with many stacked sections.
  • What tier we design at. You aim at the frontier tech tier, Amazon and OpenAI level. The brief benchmarks mainstream enterprise software.
  • Voice. You care that it stops sounding corporate and earns the developer. The brief is silent on voice. A blind spot, not a conflict.
